Introduction

At Golper Lorai, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of our platform.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect the following data when you register or participate in our competitions:

  • Full Name
  • Age
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Submitted Story Content

This data is collected through Google Forms or our website submission forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Run writing competitions
  • Identify participants
  • Contact winners or notify users about results
  • Improve our services

We do not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Google Forms & Third-Party Tools

We use Google Forms to collect submissions and Firebase to host our website. These services may store your data securely on their servers, but they do not have access to your content.

We do not use any tracking tools like Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel at this time.

4. Data Sharing Policy

We never sell or share your personal information with any third party. Your stories and contact details are stored privately and are only accessed by our team for judging or communication purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we have about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your story from the competition before judging starts

To make such a request, please contact us at: golperlorai.com@gmail.com

6. Data Security

We take data security seriously and follow reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact: golperlorai.com@gmail.com
