For Teachers – Join Golper Lorai as a Judge

Help us inspire young writers across Bangladesh.

About Golper Lorai

Golper Lorai is a national-level story-writing platform and community that encourages students and aspiring writers to express their creativity. We run monthly competitions and believe in building a strong community of readers, writers, and mentors.

Why We Invite Teachers

We believe teachers play a key role in shaping young minds.

At Golper Lorai, we don’t just need judges — we need mentors who value language, expression, and the spirit of creativity.

Join us to support and evaluate the next generation of writers.

What Judges Do

  • Read and score assigned stories (approx. 10–20 per competition)
  • Use our judging criteria (we will provide you a form or PDF)
  • Give fair and honest scores
  • Provide optional written feedback (if they want)

Benefits of Joining

  • Recognition as an official judge (on website + certificate)
  • Mention on our Facebook/Instagram
  • Help students from across Bangladesh
  • Build a stronger writing community

FAQs for Teachers

Common questions about joining our judge panel.

Q1: What kind of stories will I have to judge?

You’ll judge short or long stories written in either Bangla or English by students or amateur writers. Most are creative, thoughtful, and reviewed before reaching you.

Q2: Do I need any special training or tools?

No. We’ll provide clear judging criteria (score out of 100) and a scoring form. You only need time to read, basic judgment skills, and a love for storytelling.

Q3: How many stories do I have to judge?

Usually 10 to 20 stories per judge, depending on how many join. We try to keep it easy and fair.

Q4: Is there any payment?

Right now, this is a volunteer role. But judges will receive an official certificate, public recognition, and access to our future academic and workshop projects.

Q5: Will my name be shown publicly?

Yes — with your permission, your name, designation, and institution will be shown as part of our official judge panel (website, Facebook, etc.).

Q6: How can I stay updated about new competitions?

Once you join our teacher panel, we’ll notify you through email or WhatsApp for future events and judging opportunities.
