General Rules

  • Only one story per person per competition.
  • The story must be original ‚Äî no copy-paste or AI-generated work is allowed.
  • Accepted languages: Bangla and English only.
  • Participants must be between 13 and 50 years old.

Word Count Guidelines

  • Short Story Category: 1,000 ‚Äì 2,000 words
  • Big Story Category: 5,000 ‚Äì 8,000 words

Note: Word count must fall strictly within the specified range to be accepted for judging.

Submission Format

  • Submit via our official "Submit Your Story" button on the Home page.
  • Story should be well-formatted ‚Äî proper spelling, grammar, and paragraph breaks are expected.

Disqualification Reasons

  • Copy-pasted or plagiarized stories.
  • Using AI tools (like ChatGPT) to write stories.
  • Submitting more than one story in a single competition.
  • Promoting hate speech, adult content, or excessively violent topics.
  • Submitting work after the official deadline has passed.
