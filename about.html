Our Vision

Golper Lorai is more than just a competition — it’s a community of writers. We provide a space where people of all ages can showcase their storytelling talents, improve their craft, and get recognized. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced writer, your words have a place here.

“I believe writing is one of the purest ways to express your feelings to the world. Don’t give up — try your best every time, because every voice matters.”
Mahi Ul Alam Founder of Golper Lorai
